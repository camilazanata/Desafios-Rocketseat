Homemade Pancakes

Ingredients

1 and 1/4 cup of tea of wheat flour
1 tablespoon of sugar
3 teaspoons of baking powder
2 eggs
1 cup of tea of milk
2 tablespoons of melted butter
pinch of salt
oil

Hands On

Step 1: Mix the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in a bowl.
Step 2: In another bowl, mix the eggs, milk and butter.
Step 3: Add the liquids to the dry ones, without mixing too much.
Step 4: Mix until smooth
Step 5: Heat and grease the pan with oil, place the batter in the center.
Step 6: Flip the dough to bake on the other side and it's ready.
Tip: Serve your pancakes stacked up on a plate with a drizzle of maple syrup and any of your favourite toppings.
